About Me

Design that

Hi, I'm Haafizh Daffa — a graphic designer with a passion for building cohesive visual identities and brand experiences. I believe great design is invisible: it simply feels right.

My work spans brand identity, print design, social media content, UI/UX, and motion graphics. I approach each project with careful attention to detail and a deep understanding of the client's goals.

Based in Indonesia, I work with startups, small businesses, and individuals who want design that's not just beautiful — but purposeful.
